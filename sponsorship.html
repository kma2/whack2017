About

This semester, WHACK will take place at Wellesley College on the weekend of February 11-12, 2017. Approximately 100 undergraduates will come together for 24 hours to develop innovative web, mobile and hardware projects. WHACK aims to create a supportive environment for all students, and especially for women in tech through intimate mentor-based experiences. At WHACK, students are encouraged to explore new technologies and their creative potential.

Why sponsor?

  • Meet amazing, excited, and aspiring students!
  • Get access to resumes & portfolios
  • Present your products and APIs, and watch students unleash their creativity
  • Give your employees opportunities to mentor and inspire women students to keep pursuing opportunities in tech

How to sponsor?

  • Send engineers and designers as mentors
  • Lead workshops and fun events
  • Sponsor meals
  • Give company swag

    • We’re bringing eager, energetic participants from Wellesley and other schools to build something awesome and collaborate with each other for an entire weekend. We would love for you to meet enthusiastic women and undergrads. Join WHACK’s mentor-based experience!
